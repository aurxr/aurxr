An overview of Carroll's program

Carroll's ROTC program is dedicated to forming future leaders through Military Science courses and physical fitness classes. Our overall efforts on how we form outstanding leaders can be explained through our three defining mottos: teamwork is key, pay attention to details, and legs feed the wolf.

Students who complete all Military Science classes, as well as meets all ROTC requirements, will receive an additional minor in leadership. Because the ROTC program is an elective, students are able to pair these courses with the class required for their desired degree. Further, after graduation, students will be commissioned as an officer of the Army with a wide range of specializations.
